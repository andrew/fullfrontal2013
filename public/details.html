Hi there,

Please find below important and useful information to make sure your visit to the Full Frontal JavaScript Conference on Friday 8 November 2013 is super-duper!

Note that we've also published all these details http://full-frontal.org/details

Directions

How to get there: http://ffconf.com/directions

Full Frontal JavaScript Conference is being held at the historic Duke of York's Picturehouse in Brighton. It is centrally located on Preston Circus, less than 15 minutes walk from Brighton train station. Trains run regularly from Gatwick Airport, London Bridge and London Victoria stations. We recommend using http://www.traintimes.org.uk for up to date timetables. Hotel recommendations can be found on the Full Frontal website: http://2013.full-frontal.org/#locations

Registration

Registration opens at 9am and the conference starts at 9:40am sharp! There are no tickets for the conference, but you must bring a form of identification (bank card, driving licence etc) to pick up your name badge/programme. Please keep this with you at all times as you will need it to get back into the conference (and into the after party).

Code of Conduct

Note that by attending any of our events - workshops & conference & fringe event, you are agreeing to our code of conduct: http://confcodeofconduct.com - tl;dr: don't be a jerk.

The most up to date schedule and speaker abstracts can be found on the website: http://full-frontal.org/.

Refreshments

Tea, Coffee and Danishes will be available from 9am in the upstairs bar and the ground floor. Further refreshments will be available from the same locations at the mid morning and mid afternoon breaks. Please note lunch is not included but maps are available at registration for local recommendations, some of which can be found here: http://ffconf.com/food

Photos, videos, tweets and blog posts

Yes to all. Hashtag those bad boys with #fullfrontalconf and you're welcome to add your pictures to the Full Frontal photo group: http://www.flickr.com/groups/fullfrontal2013

After Party

Please join us at the After Party at The Loft from 7:00pm-late, maps will be available to pick up at the conference, also available here: http://ffconf.com/party (bring your pass for entry).

Pre conference

If you're coming down to Brighton on the Thursday, Async, the JavaScript Brighton meetup, are coming together for a special International Show and Tell evening. Available to all you are encouraged to sign up to give a five minute lightning talk or demo (or you can just sit back and watch). Registration is free, but please let them know if you intend to go along (so they have enough chairs!). Details can be found here: http://asyncjs.com/showntell-2013

Join us for the pre-party at The Fountainhead pub from 7pm until late: http://ffconf.com/pre-party From around 9:30pm the Full Frontal organisers and speakers, along with the Async meetup, will be heading over so be sure to say hi (but please don't think we are rude if we make our excuses and leave early!)

Health and Safety

Please report any lost property to the registration desk. Left Logic and Duke of York Picturehouse takes no responsibility for delegate's lost property so we ask you to be vigilant at all times.

The venue is fully accessible. Left Logic would like to ensure that all delegates are able to have access therefore should you have any special requests please try to contact us in advance so we can accommodate your needs.
